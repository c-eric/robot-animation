Robot 2080X

Revolutionary Technology

A new era of technology has started with the creation of a new robot named 2080X. Implemented with artificial intelligence and multiple features, it serves as a international tool for security.
This 4 frame walk cycle was made with Illustrator and Photoshop.

Assault/Defensive Mode

The 2080X robot is equipped with heavy artillery to defend or attack against intruders.
Designed in Illustrated and compiled in Photoshop.

Power Consumption

Although this robot is viewed highly due to it's revolutionary technology, it consumes a heavy amount of power to function properly.
Designed with Illustrator and animated with GSAP.
